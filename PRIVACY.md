# Privacy Policy

AutoSign does not collect, transmit, sell, or share personal data.

## Local Data

AutoSign stores the following data in the browser's extension local storage:

- Your saved signature image and normalized signature strokes.
- Signature editor settings, such as thickness and smoothing.
- Placement settings, such as field padding.

This data stays on your device unless you use browser sync, backups, or other browser features outside AutoSign.

## Reference Images

Reference images added in the signature editor are temporary underlays used for tracing. They are not stored in the saved signature.

## Website Interaction

When you choose **Sign page** and click a field, AutoSign writes your saved signature into that page. The website may then store or process the signature according to its own terms and privacy policy.

## Network Access

AutoSign does not make network requests, load remote code, or use analytics.

## Permissions

- `storage`: keeps your signature and settings locally.
- `<all_urls>` host access: allows AutoSign to run on signing pages where you choose to place a signature.
